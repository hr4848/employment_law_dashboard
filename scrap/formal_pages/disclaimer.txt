Disclaimer
The authors of this web site and sub-domains accepts no liability arising from the use of, or any part of, or reliance on the information contained within this web site, and its respective pages or downloads, opinions expressed verbally via telephone or otherwise.
The content is not intended to be legal advice, and you should not rely upon it in any respect to any specific situation you may be facing. Therefore, the content of this web site will serve as a good springboard to get professional legal advice from a solicitor.
Confidentiality
All information and documentation shared either verbally or in writing with Formal Grievance (www.formalgrievance.com) is private and confidential and will not be shared with any third parties.
Complaints
Any complaints should be brought to the attention of Sam Stone in writing via the email address on this website.
General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)/ DPA 1998
Any personal data which is sent to us is only shared with Sam & his wife Amy. Sam & Amy work as business partners.
For the avoidance of doubt, no information is shared with any third parties and/or sold on.
Emails
Sam & Amy use Proton Mail. Proton Mail uses the latest encryption technology so we can send emails and documents securely. This obviates the need to password protect documents as emails are automatically encrypted. This demonstrates our commitment to our clients to keep their personal data safe.
Cloud
Sam & Amy do not keep client’s personal data on their computers hard drive. For the avoidance of doubt, client’s personal and sensitive data is held in a cloud based system. The cloud system used by Sam & Amy is compliant under the General Data Protection Regulation.
Sensitive Personal Data
All personal / sensitive data will be deleted – (i) at your request; (ii) when you inform us that you no longer need our services, (iii) or in compliance with The General Data Protection Regulation.
Consent
In accordance with Article 7 of The General Data Protection Regulation 2018 (GDPR) the recipients (Sam & Amy) will ask for your written consent to access certain documents, which may contain your personal sensitive data. Given the necessity for the performance of the task in assisting you, we will need to process (as necessary) your personal data in order to protect your vital interests. You understand you have the right to withdraw this consent
Concerns / Complaints
Sam Stone is the point of contact should you have any concerns or questions regarding your personal data. Sam can be reached during business hours on 07775943414 or at myrightsuk@protonmail.com
Should you have a complaint, please contact Sam on the above number / email address.
